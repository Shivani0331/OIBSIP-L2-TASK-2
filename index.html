About KTR

Kalvakuntla Taraka Rama Rao (born 24 July 1976), popularly known as KTR, is a prominent Indian politician and Working President of Bharat Rashtra Samithi (BRS). As a driving force behind Telangana’s development, he has championed IT-led growth, infrastructure projects, and policies that empower youth and startups.

Education & Early Life

Studied in Hyderabad and completed higher studies abroad — brings a global perspective to state governance. Entered active politics and won his first MLA seat in 2009 from Sircilla.

Key Achievements

IT & Startups

Transformed Hyderabad into a global IT destination, built startup ecosystem (T-Hub), and attracted large investments.

Urban & Infra

Focus on metro expansion, smart city projects and urban governance for better citizen services.

Social & Women Empowerment

Initiatives like We-Hub and SHE Teams, improving entrepreneurship & safety for women.
